# Enterprise Risk Management Framework (ERMF)

A Python-based **Enterprise Risk Management Framework** developed by **Chirume Admire Tarisirayi**.

This application provides a structured, data-driven platform for identifying, assessing, monitoring, and reporting enterprise-wide risks across organisations.

## 🚀 Live Application

🔗 https://enterprise-risk-management-framework-p8exsbjtjue8cermylxgpy.streamlit.app/

---

## 💡 Overview

In many organisations, risk management remains fragmented, subjective, and compliance-driven.

This framework addresses those gaps by providing:

- Centralised risk capture and management  
- Quantitative risk scoring  
- Control effectiveness assessment  
- Residual risk computation  
- Real-time dashboards for decision-making  

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

- 📊 **Risk Identification & Register Management**  
  Capture and manage risks across departments and business units  

- 📈 **Quantitative Risk Scoring**  
  Based on Likelihood × Impact methodology  

- 🛡️ **Control Effectiveness Evaluation**  
  Incorporates strength of controls into risk reduction  

- 🔁 **Residual Risk Calculation**  
  Computes post-control exposure  

- 📉 **Risk Dashboards & Visualisation**  
  Real-time analytics using interactive charts  

- 🏢 **Enterprise-Wide Risk Aggregation**  
  Supports cross-departmental risk monitoring  

---

## 🧠 Methodology

### Inherent Risk

\[
\text{Inherent Risk} = \text{Likelihood} \times \text{Impact}
\]

### Residual Risk

\[
\text{Residual Risk} = \text{Inherent Risk} \times (1 - \text{Control Effectiveness})
\]

### Risk Classification

- Low  
- Moderate  
- High  
- Extreme
